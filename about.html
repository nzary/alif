About ALiF

What is ALiF

The AI Literacy Framework (ALiF) is an open-source, structured approach to developing AI literacy in higher education. Born from the need to systematically address AI competency development, ALiF provides clear pathways for students, faculty, and staff to master AI technologies while maintaining academic integrity.

Our Vision

To enable higher education institutions to confidently embrace AI technologies through systematic literacy development, ensuring responsible innovation and ethical implementation.
