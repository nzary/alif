ALiF
AI Literacy Framework

A Progressive Framework for AI Literacy in Higher Education

ALiF is an open-source framework designed to develop comprehensive AI literacy across higher education institutions. Built on four core competencies and three progression levels, ALiF provides structured pathways for students, faculty, and staff to master AI technologies while maintaining academic integrity.

  • Learn More

ALiF Components

Technical Understanding

Master the fundamentals of AI tools and systems through progressive skill development, from basic concepts to advanced applications.

Critical Evaluation

Develop systematic approaches to assess AI outputs, validate results, and ensure quality across all applications.

Practical Application

Transform theoretical knowledge into practical skills, implementing AI solutions effectively across academic contexts.

Impact Assessment

Measure and optimize AI integration while maintaining ethical standards and academic integrity.
